Thiruparkadal — The Cosmic Ocean of Milk

TL;DR: Thiruparkadal, the Ocean of Milk, is where Lord Vishnu rests on the serpent Adisesha, representing the eternal source of creation.

The Story

In Hindu cosmology, Thiruparkadal is the sacred ocean of milk, symbolizing purity and divinity. Here, Lord Vishnu reclines on Adisesha, watching over the universe. From this ocean arose the nectar of immortality (Amrita) during the great churning of the ocean (Samudra Manthan).

Modern Connection

Thiruparkadal is remembered as the cosmic balance — peace at the center of chaos. Even today, it inspires temple rituals, stories in art, and Golu displays during Navratri.
